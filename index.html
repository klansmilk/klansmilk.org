Milk is Racist.

In the United States, school lunch programs require milk to be offered — and often consumed — with every meal. But what they don't tell you is this: up to 80% of Black, Asian, Indigenous, and Latino populations are lactose intolerant, compared to only about 15% of white people.

Forcing dairy on children who can't digest it isn't nutrition — it's institutional harm disguised as health. We call it Klansmilk — because this practice has roots in white supremacist policy, and it continues to hurt kids today.

It's time to stop pretending that cow milk is for everyone. It's time to demand nondairy alternatives, available to all.

Join us. Speak up. Demand change. Serve better milk.
